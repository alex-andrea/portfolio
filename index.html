I am a digital UX and UI designer located in Orange County. In a world of superfluity, I believe we should surround ourselves only with what is meaningful, deliberate, and delightful — including the products and technology we use. That is why I pursue beauty in both form and function, prioritizing simplicity and efficiency in my designs.
I was born and raised in Seattle where it rains exactly as much as they say. Please hire me so I don't have to go back ;)
