Happy Elephant
personal project

Background

March 2018
Software Used:   Sketch,   Adobe Illustrator,   Adobe After Effects

Happy Elephant is a website concept I designed to aid tourists in making ethical decisions while traveling, specifically when it comes to interacting with elephants in Southeast Asia. While there are blog posts and articles listing a few noteworthy elephant sanctuaries, I couldnâ€™t find any other websites that made it easy for tourists to find organizations in the area they were travelling. The websites of the elephant sanctuaries themselves are difficult to navigate and most have outdated designs, which make them look untrustworthy. With that in mind, I designed Happy Elephant to accomplish these goals:

  • provide a layout that is easy to navigate and looks trustworthy
  • allow users to search for tours based on location
  • compile the most important information about each organization
  • provide tour information and provide links to where users can book the tours
