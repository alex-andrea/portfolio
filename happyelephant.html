Website Concept / March 2018

Happy Elephant is a website concept I designed to aid tourists in making ethical decisions while traveling, specifically when it comes to interacting with elephants in Southeast Asia.

There are existing blog posts and articles listing a few noteworthy elephant sanctuaries, but no websites I found that make it easy for tourists to find organizations in the area they are travelling. The websites of the elephant sanctuaries themselves are often difficult to navigate and/or have outdated designs, which make them look untrustworthy.

Here is the video I sampled for the landing page.

IKEA has great photos that we wanted
our design to highlight.

We used UsabilityHub to test our wireframe against a wireframe of IKEA's current layout. We gave users instructions to find a specific IKEA product. Our layout resulted in a 1779.43% increase in task completion compared to IKEA's current layout.
